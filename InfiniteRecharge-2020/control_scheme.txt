InfiniteRecharge-2020 Robot Controls

Driver Controls:
 - Drivetrain: 
    Right trigger - forward
    Left trigger  - backward
    Left joystick (horizontal axis) - steering

Operator Controls:
 - Turret
    Left Joystick (horizontal axis) - turret left/right
    Right Joystick (vertical axis) - turret up/down
    Start - Toggle Flywheel

 - Climber
    Right Trigger - scissor up
    Left Trigger  - scissor down

 - Intake/Feeder
    A - eat
    B - spit
    X - feed to turret

 - Spinner
    RB - spin right
    LB - spin left
